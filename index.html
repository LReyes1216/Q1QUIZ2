Spider-Man: Across the Spiderverse

  • Spider-Man: Across the Spiderverse is a movie released on June 2, 2023
  • It is a sequel to the movie, Spider-Man: Into the Spiderverse
  • The story continues Miles' story as he tries to stop the villain, Spot,
    while he tries to save his father in the process.
  • There will be a sequel called "Spider-man: Beyond the Spiderverse" coming soon.
    Unfortunately, this will be the end of the trilogy.
